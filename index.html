How are we vulnerable ?

The Social Vulnerability Index is the relative social vulnerability of census tracts and counties across the U.S. The index is a composite of the 15 variables, each variable is based on demographic information collected by the Census.

Overall Vulnerability Score
This is how the Social Vulnerability Index describes New York City. Census tracts in the Bronx has some of the highest overall vulnerability scores in New York City and in the country.
